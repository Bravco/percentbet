# PercentBet

**%bet** is an AI-powered SaaS web app, which spots mispriced prediction markets, determine the most likely outcome and gives real odds.

You can try [PercentBet](https://percentbet.com) for free!

## Disclaimer

PercentBet does not provide financial, investment, trading, betting, or legal advice. The outputs generated by the platform represent algorithmic estimates and opinions, not guarantees of outcomes. Markets are inherently uncertain, and actual results may differ significantly from any predictions or probabilities shown.
